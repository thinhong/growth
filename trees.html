TOOL 03 · DECISIONS

Fast & frugal trees.

Fast-and-frugal trees ask three or four binary questions in sequence, and stop the moment an answer settles the matter. They outperform long deliberations because most decisions are not, in fact, complicated — we only pretend they are to delay them.

FORMATBINARY WALK DURATION~2 MIN LIBRARY1 / 3
▶ How to walk a tree
Pick a tree. Bring one specific situation in mind — not a category, a situation. Walk the questions in order; do not skip ahead to the answer you want. The tree's verdict is binding for ninety seconds: if you still disagree after that, the disagreement is the data.
